The 334 Manipur Maktab was established in 1932 and is managed by the Department of Education. It is a co-educational school in a rural area of Raja Bazar block, Cachar district, Assam. The school offers grades 1 to 5 with a pre-primary section. Bengali is the medium of instruction and the academic session starts in April. The school has a government building with three classrooms and two non-teaching rooms in good condition. There is a separate room for the head teacher. The school has a boundary wall but no electricity connection. Tap water is the source of drinking water and there are functional toilets for both boys and girls. The school has a library with 15 books but no playground, computers or computer-aided learning lab. Mid-day meals are provided and prepared on the school premises.

Teachers

They are highly educated professionals who are experts in their respective fields, and they possess a deep understanding of the curriculum and learning objectives.
